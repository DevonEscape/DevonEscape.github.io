What is an Escape Room?

You are locked in a room for 60 minutes. You have to solve a series of puzzles and mental challenges, working as a team in order to escape.

How much does it cost?

¬£20 per player

Is it difficult?

Well it certainly isn’t impossible! The games are designed to be challenging and thought provoking, but also great fun. You only have 60 minutes though, so the pressure is on to escape in time!

How old do I have to be?

There is no age restriction, however the puzzles may be too demanding for those under 12 so they may not get the full experience from the game. We do ask that children under 16 are accompanied by an adult.

What is the minimum / maximum team size?

There is a minimum team size of 2 and our games support a maximum of 6 players.

Are there parking facilities available?

There is no on-site parking at the premises. We would recommend parking in the nearby Cricketfield car park and then walking to us – we are only a 4 minute walk away!

I am travelling on the train – How far are you from the train station?

We are only seven minutes walk away from Newton Abbot train station. Located in behind Enterprise rent a car.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payment through our booking system as well as most major debit and credit cards on the day.

Can I take photos or videos inside the room?

We have a strict policy of no mobile phones or cameras to be used whilst in the rooms. However we do take a photo at the end of the session of your team either within the room or in the reception area. Your choice.

Can I race my friends or work colleagues in the other room?

We can accommodate a “race” style game between two groups. Please let us know before you book if you wish for this to happen and we will do our best to arrange this.

I have a group of more than 12 – what can you do for me?

We can accommodate groups of more than 12 over several time slots. We would ask that you contact us on bookings@escaperoomsdevon.co.uk to make further arrangements.
