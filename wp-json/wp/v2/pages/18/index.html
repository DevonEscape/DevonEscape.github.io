D-Day for Devon<\/strong><\/h2>\n

The year is 1942.<\/p>\n

Following occupation of the Channel Islands, a resistance fighter has sent information of an imminent German invasion within the local vicinity.<\/p>\n

Help the Home Guard ascertain the exact location in time to protect Devon from German occupation.<\/p>\n
