Privacy Policy<\/h1>\n

This policy explains how we comply with European and UK legislation\u00a0to protect your identity and privacy. It explains how we collect, use and disclose certain information.<\/span><\/p>\n

<\/h2>\n

Company Details<\/h2>\n

Devon Escape is a UK based escape room, we are located at The Workshop, Lemon Place, Newton Abbot, Devon, TQ12 2BD. Company No. 10810951<\/p>\n

Tel: 01626 368980
\nEmail:\u00a0bookings@escaperoomsdevon.co.uk<\/a><\/p>\n

\u200b<\/span><\/h2>\n

Data Provided to us when booking a room<\/h2>\n

\u200b<\/span>Data you provide to Devon Escape is collected when you enter in to an agreement with Devon Escape\u2019s services and internally only. This data is not passed on to marketing agencies:<\/span><\/p>\n

    \n
  • \u200b<\/span>Your name<\/span><\/li>\n
  • Your address (optional on booking)<\/span><\/li>\n
  • Your email addresses<\/span><\/li>\n
  • Your telephone number\/s<\/span><\/li>\n
  • Your payment method<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    <\/h2>\n

    Data collected automatically by Devon Escape<\/h2>\n
      \n
    • Date and time of booking<\/span><\/li>\n
    • Number in a group<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

      <\/h2>\n

      Use Of Data<\/h2>\n

      \u200b<\/span>If when you booked, you ticked the box authorising for us to use keep your details then we will use this data for the following:<\/span><\/p>\n

        \n
      • To let you know of any new rooms or changes to rooms that might be of interest to you<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

        If when you booked, you did not tick the box authorising for us to use keep your details then we will use this data for the following:<\/span><\/p>\n

          \n
        • Our accounts – \u00a0which must be legally sent to the government<\/span><\/li>\n
        • Your telephone numbers and emails in case we need to contact you before your booking, for example in the unfortunate instance where we need to cancel<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

          Please note that unless requested, your data will be deleted after your game has been completed.<\/span><\/p>\n

          <\/h2>\n

          Access Of Data<\/h2>\n

          Your data is securely stored upon password protected computers only accessible by authorised Devon Escape staff. You can request access to data we hold about you, this can be done via email or telephone call from an authorised person.<\/span><\/p>\n

          To request, modify or delete any data that we may hold on you please send requests to\u00a0bookings@escaperoomsdevon.co.uk<\/a>.<\/span><\/p>\n
